Sleigh Ride Dinners

An event of a lifetime that you won’t forget. Our dinner sleighs in Telluride are truly a one of a kind opportunity. Ride a horse-pulled sleigh through the mountains where you’ll land at our private event center in Telluride. You and your guests will be served a customized four-course meal in a heated tent. We offer two different sleigh ride times each night, and the entire event lasts approximately three hours.

For this event, Chef Graeme Charles partners with Telluride Sleighs. For more information on dinner sleighs and booking, click below.
